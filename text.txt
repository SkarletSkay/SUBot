description:

Hello! This bot is designed to facilitate connection between you and the Student Union (SU). This bot can help you to:

send a request to SU about your idea of university's life improvement
send a request to SU about your project
send a complaint to SU
check the status of the request


SU core options:

answer for a particular request
view all active requests
view all closed requests
view all requests
update the status of the request
decline/accept the request for a project
redirect the request to another person (representative, coordinator, mentor)


after the new request:

Your request was successfully sent to SU!


different statuses:

Your request was sent to SU
SU is working on your request
The request was closed
